Creamy Tomato and Chicken Pasta

Description:

An easy creamy tomato sauce you can make at home in no time at all.

This dish takes 25 minutes, can serve your whole family and is easy to add you own twists to.

Ingredients:

  • 1lb diced chicken or ground beef
  • Pasta of your choice
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • Heavy cream
  • Jarlic
  • Seasonigns of your choice
  • Oil
  • (optional) cooked vegetables

Steps:

  1. Boil a pot of water and heat up a pan on medium heat
  2. When the water is boiling start cooking your pasta
  3. When the pan is hot add some oil and Jarlic
  4. When the jarlic is golden brown, add your protein with any seasoning you like
  5. When the pasta is cooked, strain it, saving a ladle full of pasta water
  6. Add tomato paste to your protein and break it up as much as possible
  7. Add in the cream and mix to your liking
  8. Throw in the pasta with the pasta water and lower the heat, mix until all coated
