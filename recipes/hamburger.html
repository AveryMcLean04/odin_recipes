Hamburger

Description:

You all know what a hamburger is.

Ingredients:

  • Hamburger patty
  • Hamburger bun
  • Condiments
  • Tomato
  • Red onion
  • Lettuce

Steps:

  1. Cook the burger patty on a pan or a grill if you have one
  2. While it is cooking, slice up your vegetables so they can be put onto the burger and set aside
  3. (optional) right before the burger is done cooking start toasting your bun so it is toasted around when the burger is finished cooking
  4. Remove the burger from the heat and plate with condiments and toppings
