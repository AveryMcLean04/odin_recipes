Stir-Fry

Description:

A great way to mix up your standard beef, rice and vegetables while staying healthy.

Ingredients:

  • White rice
  • Beef
  • Soy sauce
  • Vegetables

Steps:

  1. Start cooking your rice in a pot or a rice cooker, wait until it is almost done
  2. Slice up your beef into small strips or pieces that don't need to be cut
  3. Cut up your veggies to your liking
  4. In a large pan on medium heat, start cooking your beef to your liking
  5. Once it is almost finished, add in your vegetables, monitor and stir every few minutes
  6. When everything is cooked, add in the rice and some soy sauce and stir until thoroughly mixed
